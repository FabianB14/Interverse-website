Own Your Gaming Universe

Interverse is an SDK for game developers that brings true cross-game asset ownership and portability to the $321 billion gaming industry. Transfer digital items between games while preserving their value and utility — with a drop-in integration for Unity, Unreal Engine, and RPG Maker.

Support Our Mission Investor Information

What We're Building

Interverse creates a universal layer for asset portability, solving the $74 billion trapped digital asset problem.

🎮

True Ownership

Players actually own the items they earn and buy. Assets stay with the player even if a single game shuts down.

🔄

Cross-Game Transfers

Seamlessly transfer items between different games while our protocol preserves their relative value and utility.

💰

Investment Preservation

Keep the value of your time and money investments when moving to new games rather than starting from scratch.

🔧

10-Line Integration

Game developers can integrate with just 10-15 lines of code using our SDKs for Unity, Unreal Engine, and RPG Maker.

📈

Sustainable Revenue

Developers earn ongoing revenue from transaction fees whenever their assets are traded or used across games.

⚡

Gaming-Optimized Backend

Purpose-built infrastructure with 10,000+ TPS, sub-second confirmations, and minimal fees — tuned specifically for real-time gameplay.
