Problem & Solution

We're solving the $74 billion trapped digital asset problem.

The Problem

  • Asset Silos: Players spend billions on in-game items that remain trapped within individual games.
  • Lost Value: When players move to new games, their investments of time and money are effectively lost.
  • Lack of Ownership: Despite spending real money, players don't truly own their digital assets.
  • Integration Barriers: Most game developers lack the technical expertise to implement blockchain.
  • Performance Limitations: Existing blockchain solutions suffer from high fees and slow confirmation times.

Our Solution

  • Custom Gaming Blockchain: Purpose-built for gaming with 10,000+ TPS and sub-second confirmations.
  • Universal Asset Protocol: Standardized layer that translates assets between different game environments.
  • Developer SDK Suite: Simple integration for Unity, Unreal Engine, and custom solutions.
  • Cross-Game Marketplace: Secure trading platform for assets across game boundaries.
  • Balanced Governance: IVX token gives both players and developers a voice in the platform's evolution.

How It Works

Our technology enables seamless cross-game asset interoperability.

1

Asset Tokenization

In-game assets are tokenized on the Interverse blockchain, creating a digital certificate of ownership that exists independently of any single game.

2

Universal Asset Protocol

Our proprietary protocol standardizes assets across different game environments, preserving their relative value and utility when transferred between games.

3

Developer Integration

Game developers integrate our SDK with as little as 10-15 lines of code, allowing their games to recognize and utilize Interverse assets.

4

Cross-Game Transfer

Players can transfer their assets between supported games, with our protocol automatically handling the conversion to maintain value and functionality.

5

Value Preservation

Assets retain and can even increase in value over time as they become usable across more games, creating a sustainable ecosystem for both players and developers.
